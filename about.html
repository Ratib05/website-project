Biography

Born in clayton, melbourne, I moved to sydney after one year in melbourne. at the age of 4, I began preschool at a childcare center near my house and started kindergarten in when i turned five. Soon I was diagnosed with a genetic disease called Mitochondrial disease, which affects my height, strength, stamina and cognitive (mental) ability. Throughout my school years, my school did it's best to cater to my needs. I successfully graduated high school at the end of 2022 at the time I was forecasted to. I recieved the Reuben F. scarf award for hard work and diligence despite my difficulties and enrolled to study cybersecurity and behaviour at western sydney university.

In uni and prior, I have developed several projects displaying my abilities with video editing and coding. I have made games in several programming laguages such as python and java and I have done video editing of gaming clips in video editors called VN video editor can capcut.

Life's balance is all about the right mix of button mashing in gaming, pumping iron at the gym, diving into the depths of captivating books, and exploring the complex worlds of anime and manga. The adrenaline rush from defeating the final boss, the satisfaction of lifting more weight in the gym, the thrill of discovering a hidden literary gem, and the emotional rollercoaster from following the epic and emotional journeys of beloved anime characters, all contribute to the harmony of my day to life. Each of these hobbies adds a unique flavor to my routine, making each day an exciting adventure filled with a blend of physical betterment, mental stimulation, and immersive storytelling.
